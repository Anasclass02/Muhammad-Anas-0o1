Video Editer

A video editor is a visual storyteller who manipulates and combines video files to create a finished product. The role involves reviewing raw footage, choosing the best takes, and layering audio, music, and graphics to ensure a seamless flow that conveys a specific message or emotion.

Web development

Web development is the technical process of building and maintaining websites. It ranges from "Front-end" development (designing the visual layout and user interface using code) to "Back-end" development (managing the server, database, and logic that powers the site's functionality).

YouTuber

A YouTuber is a content strategist and brand manager who produces video content specifically for the YouTube platform. This role requires a blend of audience research, search engine optimization (SEO) for video titles and tags, community engagement, and the ability to analyze performance metrics to grow a channel’s reach.

MS Word

Microsoft Word is the industry standard for word processing and professional documentation. Proficiency in this tool involves creating, formatting, and editing text-based documents such as reports, resumes, and formal letters. It includes using advanced features like styles, track changes, mail merge, and document security.
